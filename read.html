Case Title : Jone vs Jane

Jurisdiction : High Court

Date of Judgment : 23-11-2009

Case Summary : Matrix Business Consulting, Inc. (Matrix) entered into a contract with Gregory Manufacturing Company (Gregory) to provide consulting services aimed at improving Gregory's manufacturing processes. Matrix alleged that Gregory breached the contract by failing to pay for services rendered. In response, Gregory contended that Matrix did not deliver the agreed-upon services satisfactorily, thereby justifying the non-payment.
